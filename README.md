### Create a JDBC CRUD application to work with Pokemons and Trainers
#### What to do:
- CRUD over Pokemons
- CRUD over trainers

- One pokemon belongs to one or no trainers
- One trainer can have multiple pokemon

#### Special features your app offers
- List the pokemons that belong to a given trainer
- List trainers that are sorted by who has how many pokemons
- List the Pokemons that are not caught (do not belong to anyone)
- Catch a Pokemon (select a trainer who catches, then a Pokemon that doesn't belong to anyone)
